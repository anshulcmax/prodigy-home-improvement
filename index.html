Frequently Asked Questions

Does my roof need full replacement or just repair? +
Our team provides a thorough roof inspection to assess the extent of any damage. If the roof is under 15 years old and the damage is localized, repair is often the right choice. If shingles are widely deteriorated or the roof is aging significantly, full roof replacement is typically more cost-effective long-term. We will always give you an honest assessment not a sales pitch.
Do you assist with insurance claims for storm damage? +
Yes. We have extensive experience guiding homeowners through property insurance claims for hail damage and wind damage. We document all damage thoroughly to support your claim and can communicate with insurance adjusters on your behalf throughout the process.
What does being a GAF Certified Contractor mean for me? +
GAF Certification means our installers meet strict standards for training and installation quality set by the largest roofing manufacturer in North America. It also means your new roof qualifies for GAF’s enhanced warranty program providing coverage that non-certified contractors cannot offer.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve Cumming, GA (Forsyth County) and the surrounding North Metro Atlanta area including Sugar Hill, Suwanee, Johns Creek, Alpharetta, Dawsonville, Gainesville, and Buford. Call us to confirm availability for your specific location.
Are all projects owner-supervised? +
Yes every project is supervised by the owner from start to finish. This is a commitment we make to every client, ensuring the highest standards of quality control and accountability on your property. No subcontractors, no surprises.
